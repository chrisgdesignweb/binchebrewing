Brussels Pale Ale


ABV: 6.8%

Malts: Pilsner, Wheat, Biscuit

Hops: First Gold (UK)

Yeast: La Chouffe

Serving: Bottle Conditioned

Description: Belgian-style Pale Ale w/ Corriander

Tasting Notes:Crystal clear and nice carbonation from the bottle conditioning. Spicy with a touch of clove from the yeast. Good malt biscuity profile with a touch of citrus from the First Gold Hops. Really drinkable for the higher ABV, finished really dry.
