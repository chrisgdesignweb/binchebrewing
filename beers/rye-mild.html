Ruby Rye Mild


ABV: 3.5%

Malts: Pale, Crystal 80, Chocolate, Flaked Rye

Hops: Nugget

Yeast: Vermont

Serving: Draft

Description: English Mild brewed with Rye and fermented out with Vermont Yeast.

Tasting Notes:Freshly baked bread comes to mind in the aroma with malt dominating this beer. Very drinkable with subtle spice and caremel sweetness rounds out a chewy mouthfeel for such a small beer. Touch of fruitiness from the yeast, very drinkable with alot of flavor. Great lacing.
