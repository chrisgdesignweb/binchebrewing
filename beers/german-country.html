German Country Ale


ABV: 5%

Malts: Pilsner, Munich, Wheat

Hops: Sterling

Yeast: German Ale

Serving: Draft

Description: German Kölsch-style beer brewed in winter at low temps

Tasting Notes:Bready from the Pils maly but a huge floral and herbal note from the large dry hop on this. Highly drinkable and could pass for a faux-pilsner. I really enjoyed using the Fermentis Safale K-97 yeast in this beer. Good yeast for winter brewing clean beers.
