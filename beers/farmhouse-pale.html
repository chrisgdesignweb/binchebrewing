Farmhouse Pale Ale


ABV: 3.7%

Malts: Pilsner, Wheat, Crystal 75

Hops: Homegrown Cascades

Yeast: Duvel

Serving: Bottle Conditioned

Description: Second Runnings ofa pilsner w/crystal added to the mash and homegrown cascade hops.

Tasting Notes:Very yeast driven with a citrusy lemon flavor going on. There's a real subtle bitterness from the Cascade hops and great lacing on the glass. There's a touch of apple and pear in the nose that reminds of a mini-Duvel - probably from the yeast. Bready pils malt profile.
