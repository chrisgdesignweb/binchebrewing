Amarillo Rising


ABV: 4.6%

Malts: Pilsner

Hops: Amarillo

Yeast: Vermont

Serving: Draft

Description: Based of of one of The Alchemist beers this is a nice simple Blonde ale.

Tasting Notes: Bready pils malt matched with notes of orange, tangerine and some apricot from the yeast. Got a really great dank note from the dry hop which was a actually a dry tea hop in the keg.
