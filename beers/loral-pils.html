Loral Pilsner


ABV: 4.5%

Malts: Pilsner, Wheat

Hops: Lorel

Yeast: Hurlimann Swiss Lager

Serving: Draft

Description: German style pilsner brewed with 100% Loral Hops

Tasting Notes: Bready pils malt matched with super floral almost noble-like hop presence. Beer is crystal clear with a nice golden hue. Swiss lager yeast produced a clean hop focused beer. Lagered for 3 months.
