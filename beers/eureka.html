Sunshine Pale Ale: Eureka


ABV: 5%

Malts: Pilsner, Pale, Wheat, Flaked Oats, Munich

Hops: Columbus, Eureka

Yeast: Vermont

Serving: Draft

Description: New England focused Pale ale with a ton of late addition Eureka Hops

Tasting Notes: Tropical, Citrus (Sweet Tangerine) and a touch of dank resin. My favorite new modern hop. Silky mouthfeel from the oats and small apricot/peach flavors from the Vermont Yeast. This is by far one of my favorite beers of 2018.
